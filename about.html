Our Story

From bean to cup with passion

Brew Haven since 2010

We're a family-owned coffee shop dedicated to ethical sourcing and community.

Our journey began with a simple idea: create a space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee while feeling at home. We travel the world to source our beans directly from small farms, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices.

Every cup is roasted in small batches in our micro-roastery right here in Coffeeville. We believe in highlighting the unique flavors of each origin, whether it's a fruity Ethiopian natural or a chocolatey Colombian washed.

100% Ethical Sourcing

We work directly with farmers, paying fair prices and building long-term relationships.

Recognized for best coffee, best atmosphere, and community impact.
